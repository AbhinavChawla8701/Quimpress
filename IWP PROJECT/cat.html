Wikipedia-Geneva

Geneva is the second most populous city in Switzerland(after Zurich) and is the most populous city of Romandy(the french speaking part of switzerland) situated where Rhone riverexits lake Geneva(French lac leyman).It is the capital of republic and cartoon of geneva.

Geneva is the global capital and a financial centre, and a worldwide centre for diplomacy due to the presence of numerous international organizations, including the headquarters of many agencies of the United Nations and the Red Cross. Geneva hosts the highest number of internationalcombatants and prisoners of war. Together with, for instance, New York City (global headquarters of the UN), Basel (Bank for International Settlements), and

Strasbourg (Council of Europe), Geneva is a city serving as the headquarters of one of the most important international organizations, without being the capital of a country.
